How this morning sheet works

This is Part 1 of the Tongue–Rhythm method. In the morning, you gently observe your tongue and basic rhythm. In the evening, you complete the Evening Rhythm Score. Together they create a simple daily rhythm awareness practice.

For best results, try this for 3 days in a row. No need to change anything big at first — simply record and observe.
